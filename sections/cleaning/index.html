Cleaning Awareness

Maintain hygiene and follow procedures to ensure a safe work environment.

🧼 General Cleaning Guidelines

  • 🧽 Clean the floor on a daily basis.
  • ❄️ Clean the air conditioner when found dirty.
  • 🪰 Check the EFK (Electronic Fly Killer); if not clean, report to the store in-charge.
  • 📦 Wrap film rolls properly; if found open, secure them immediately.
  • 🗑️ If raw material spills, dispose of it in the Food Waste bin.
  • 🚮 Dispose of non-food waste in the Non-Food Waste bin.
  • 🧴 Sanitize high-touch surfaces regularly (door handles, switches, etc.).
  • 🧤 Always wear gloves while cleaning sensitive areas.

🔴🔵 Allergen vs Non-Allergen Cleaning Tools

Allergen Products

Use Red Mop 🧹 and Red Broom 🧼 for cleaning.

Non-Allergen Products

Use Blue Mop 🧹 and Blue Broom 🧼 for cleaning.

🗑️ Waste Disposal

  • Dispose spilled raw materials in the food waste bin.
  • Dispose packaging, gloves, and other waste in the non-food waste bin.
  • Always cover bins and keep the waste area clean.

✨ Additional Hygiene Tips

  • Use designated cleaning equipment for different zones.
  • Replace old or damaged tools promptly.
  • Train staff regularly on updated cleaning protocols.
