Understand and identify allergens in our workplace to ensure food safety.

🧪 Allergens

🚨 Common Allergens We Monitor

Peanuts 🥜

Highly allergenic. Must be separated from other ingredients and clearly labeled.

Dairy 🥛

Milk and milk-based products must be stored and handled with care to avoid cross-contact.

Gluten (Wheat) 🌾

Label all gluten-containing items and avoid using shared equipment with gluten-free products.

✅ Allergen Handling Tips

  • Wash hands and change gloves between handling different food items.
  • Use separate utensils and cutting boards for allergenic and non-allergenic foods.
  • Label all ingredients clearly and double-check recipes before production.
  • Store allergens on separate shelves to avoid contamination.
  • Train staff regularly on allergen awareness and safety procedures.
